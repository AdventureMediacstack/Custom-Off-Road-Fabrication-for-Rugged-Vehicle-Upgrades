FAQ

Q1: What is off road fabrication?
 Off road fabrication is the process of customizing or building vehicle components, like bumpers, skid plates, and roof racks, to improve durability, performance, and utility for rugged terrain.

Q2: Why should I invest in custom off road fabrication?
 Custom fabrication ensures your vehicle is tailored to your adventure needs, with stronger components, better cargo solutions, and improved trail protection.

Q3: Can off road fabrication be done on older vehicles?
 Yes. Classic and vintage vehicles, including Scout IIs and other off-road rigs, can be upgraded with modern fabrication for enhanced performance and durability.

Q4: What types of modifications are included in off road fabrication?
 Typical upgrades include custom bumpers, rock sliders, skid plates, roof racks, and lighting mounts, all built to fit your specific vehicle and adventure requirements.

Q5: How long does the fabrication process take?
 The timeline varies based on complexity but typically includes consultation, design, fabrication, and installation. Experienced shops can provide estimates during your initial consultation.

Q6: Is off road fabrication worth it for overlanding?
 Absolutely. Fabricated components like roof racks, reinforced bumpers, and skid plates help carry gear safely and protect your vehicle on long-distance trails and rough terrain.
